Better Baba Ganoush (Baigan Choka)

Source: Adapted from Ottolenghiâ€™s Plenty More

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium eggplants (about 1 kg)
  • 1 mild red chili or squirt of good hot sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tbsp neutral oil
  • small onion thinly sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • chopped chives or chinese garlic chives
  • salt (to taste)
  • lemon juice (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. broil eggplant, occasionaly turning, until skin is charred and plant is soft, about 1 hour. If using chili, broil chili as well, about 10 min. Let cool and remove flesh.
  2. Heat oils, add onion, Fry on high until slightly crispy.
  3. Blend everything together using a handblender.
