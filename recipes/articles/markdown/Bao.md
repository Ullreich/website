# Bao Bun Recipe
[source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OFt3SThGi7M) 

## Tips
* make a double batch (at least)

## Ingredients
* 300g flour
* 40g sugar
* 2 tsp/6g yeast
* 185ml warm milk
* 1 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. knead together ingredients until smooth (8 min in machine)
2. rest for 2h
3. make into bao
4. steam for 15-18 min
